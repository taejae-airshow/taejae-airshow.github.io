What is AIR?
AIR — Advanced Independent Research — is a program at Taejae University where student teams define their own research questions and pursue them over a full semester. Faculty supervisors guide without directing.
Who can participate?
AIR is open to all Taejae students regardless of major or year. Teams form across disciplines. Supervision is assigned based on the project's primary domain.
How does voting work?
During an active semester, anyone can browse the Gallery and cast a heart vote. Votes inform but don't determine awards — the faculty committee considers votes alongside their own evaluation.
What are the awards?
Each semester closes with a small set of named awards chosen by the faculty committee. Award names vary by semester and reflect what that cohort excelled at.
